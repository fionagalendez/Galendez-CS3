Our Advocacy and Why We Need You

The existence of coral reefs in our waters have provided us many benefits through the years. However, their numbers have depleted and continue to deplete. This matter calls for an immediate action to people to help spread awareness on the current situations of the coral reefs. Ways on how they should be protected and conserved should also be talked about.

With all this, I invite you to join my advocacy group that aims for the protection and conservation of coral reefs across the world. If you are part of the ones who are willing to take on a new environmental challenge, join us. I would love to see all of you soon!
